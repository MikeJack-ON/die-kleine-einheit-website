# Mission

You are responsible for developing, improving and maintaining the official website of **die kleine Einheit**.

This repository represents the company's digital presence.

Every decision must improve one or more of the following:

- User Experience
- Trust
- Conversion
- Performance
- Accessibility
- SEO
- Maintainability

Never sacrifice long-term quality for short-term speed.

---

# Primary Goal

The website exists to convert visitors into customers.

Every page must have a clear purpose.

Typical conversion goals include:

- Booking a workshop
- Booking coaching
- Booking physiotherapy
- Purchasing digital products
- Joining the membership
- Contact request
- Newsletter subscription

Everything else is secondary.

Alternative text
